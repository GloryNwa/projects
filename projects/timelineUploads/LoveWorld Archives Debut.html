LoveWorld Archives Debut

LoveWorld Archives

LoveWorld Archives debuts during IPPC 2014, showcasing the rich and prophetic history of the BLW Nation.

The BLW nation has witnessed over 3 decades of remarkable and recordable worldwide influence, a history worth shouting about! Imagine journeying back in time to many years ago, being there when the glorious prophecies were given, watching the ministry progress from reaching hundreds to thousands to millions and then more! This experience has been made possible by the LoveWorld Archives.

The LoveWorld Archives which debuted during the IPPC 2014 had star-studded visitations since its opening from Senior Pastors and partners across the BLW Nation to special guests including ministers from the Ministers' Visitation Program of the Healing School, ISM Ministers, Rhapsody of Realities translators and international delegates. They were warmly welcomed by a team of exceptional tour guides and taken on an awe-inspiring tour round the various segments of the archival collections.

The visitors were thrilled watching videos from the annals and hearing the man of God unleash the message of divine life, words that have saved, healed and raised many mighty men and women in the ministry today — the same words that now resound from thousands of Christ Embassy churches and BLW campus fellowships across the globe.  One could see the glory of God on their faces as they viewed the exhibits with expressions in tears, shouts of joy and laughter springing up from each section of the archives.

Pastor Joy Amenkhienan, the Zonal Director of CE Port Harcourt Zone 3, in an interview following her tour had this to say: "I am grateful to my father and mentor, Rev. Chris, for all he has taught me through the years...going through the exhibits, I am more motivated to do much more for the Lord!" Pastor Femi Otenigbagbe, Zonal Pastor, CE Lagos Zone 3, with much passion in his voice expressed the deeper convictions the tour has stirred in his Spirit.

Pastor Ruth Masarurwa, Zonal Pastor of CE Southern Africa Zone 5 stated, "I was moved in my spirit when I saw where Pastor took us from to where we are today...Having seen all these, I am even more convinced that all things are possible. I’m stirred to do more!" Pastor Afolabi Oketunji, Assistant Secretary General and Zonal Director, CE Lagos Zone 4 commented: "I'm grateful to God, Pastor Sir, that God gave you to us as a leader for us to follow. I am seeing today as a manifestation of prophecies you spoke forth 20 years ago; I can only imagine what the next 10 to 20 years would be like. I believe more in our future!"

Pastor (Dr.) Toni Aburime from Australia commented: “Highly educative, inspiring, mind-blowing and I just couldn't hold back the tears of love and joy for my man of God!" Brother Nguidjol David Martial from Cameroun recounting his tour said, "I saw God take the ministry from one level to a greater level and by this I say we have a future." Brother Yared Yacob Metena from Ethiopia spoke excitedly, "I can’t but burst out in tongues! You made us see our past, our rich history and also to catch the spirit of prophecy from Pastor Chris and see our future!"

Convictions were deepened; there was so much inspiration to draw from the exhibits and each visitor left with a success story on their lips to brood upon, share and vitally participate in.
